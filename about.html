The GH. L GRECO LTD was founded in 2014 and is based in Cyprus with its main and primary object that of Greek Traditional Products trade. Thanks to its vision, creativity, consistency and accountability, our company aspires to become internationally renowned in showcasing the treasures of the Greek land through carefully selected products.


Standing by our logo “passion for quality“, we offer and promote products with high nutritional value and excellent quality at competitive prices capable to satisfy even the most demanding customer. This is the philosophy and spirit that pervades the company, which we serve with consistency and dedication.
